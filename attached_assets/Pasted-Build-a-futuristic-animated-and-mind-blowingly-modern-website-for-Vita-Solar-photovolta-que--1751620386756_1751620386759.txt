Build a **futuristic, animated, and mind-blowingly modern website** for **Vita Solar photovoltaïque**, a solar energy company based in Hammamet, Tunisia.

The design should be inspired by https://us.sunpower.com in terms of clean structure and professionalism, but enhanced with **Framer Motion animations**, **Lottie icons**, **glassmorphism**, and **futuristic UI effects** to make it feel alive, dynamic, and forward-thinking.

  4. 🔋 Solutions de stockage

💡 Design Style:
- Clean grid layout (like SunPower)
- Use **Framer Motion** for scroll animations, reveal effects, hover microinteractions
- Use **Lottie animations** for icons (solar panel, battery, tools, sun)
- **Glassmorphism cards** for service blocks (translucent, blur, soft border)
- **Glow buttons** with gradient borders & ripple click effect
- Include **animated gradient backgrounds** and **parallax solar particles** in hero or sections
- Use **custom cursor** with hover trails or magnetic CTA effects
- Add **dark mode toggle** with sun → moon morph animation
- Include **floating geometric shapes or animated solar waves** in background layers
